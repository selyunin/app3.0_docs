.. _BST_SHUTTLE_BOARD_CONNECTOR:

:ref:`BST_SHUTTLE_BOARD_CONNECTOR`
#####################################

This asymmetric 7-9 pin connector is used to plug-in the shuttle sensor boards into the application board 3.0.
Asymmetry serves the purpose to eliminate the possibility of wrong connection.


.. table:: **BST_SHUTTLE_BOARD_CONNECTOR**

    +-----------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
    | **Short Side**                          | **Long Side**                           |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **Pin number**     | **Signal / Line**  | **Pin number**     | **Signal / Line**  |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **1**              | VDD                | **1**              | CS                 |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **2**              | VDDIO              | **2**              | SCK/SCL            |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **3**              | GND                | **3**              | SDO                |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **4**              | GPIO0              | **4**              | SDI/SDA            |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **5**              | GPIO1              | **5**              | GPIO4              |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **6**              | GPIO2/INT1         | **6**              | GPIO5              |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **7**              | GPIO3/INT2         | **7**              | IOXP_INT           |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    |                    |                    | **8**              | PlugDet            |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    |                    |                    | **9**              | PROM RW            |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+



.. _SWD:

:ref:`SWD`
#####################################

The 10-pin ARM SWD connector allows connecting e.g. 9-pin Cortex M adapter from `Segger`_.

.. _Segger: https://www.segger.com/products/debug-probes/j-link/accessories/adapters/9-pin-cortex-m-adapter/

.. table:: **SWD Connector pin-out**

    +-----------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
    | **Left Side**                           | **Right Side**                          |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **Pin number**     | **Signal / Line**  | **Pin number**     | **Signal / Line**  |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **1**              | 1.8V               | **2**              | SWDIO              |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **3**              | GND                | **4**              | SWDCLK             |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **5**              | GND                | **6**              | SWDO               |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **7**              | Not connected      | **8**              | Not connected      |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **9**              | GND                | **10**             | MCU RESET          |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+


.. _BCDS-XDK:

:ref:`BCDS-XDK`
#####################################

The connector is located on the opposite side of the board, and can be used to connect the battery.
You need to solder to the provided points.

.. table:: **BCDS-XDK**

    +-----------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
    | **Short Side**                          | **Long Side**                           |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **Pin number**     | **Signal / Line**  | **Pin number**     | **Signal / Line**  |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **1**              | GPIO5              | **2**              | AIN2 XDK           |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **3**              | GPIO4              | **4**              | AIN3 XDK           |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **5**              | SPO0 MISO          | **6**              | GPIO0              |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **7**              | SPI0 MOSI          | **8**              | GPIO1              |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **9**              | SPI0 CS            | **10**             | GPIO EXT1 XDK      |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **11**             | SPI0 SCK           | **12**             | GPIO EXT2 XDK      |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **13**             | 2.8V               | **14**             | 1.8V               |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **15**             | 2.8V               | **16**             | 1.8V               |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **17**             | BAT+               | **18**             | BAT-               |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
    | **19**             | BAT+               | **20**             | BAT-               |
    +--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+--------------------+
